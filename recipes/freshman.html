College Freshman

Description

As a bumbling mess coming out of high school, the Freshman is the evolution of the HS variety's Senior. It is easily identifiable in the campus habitat by its wide-eyed confusion and the Google Maps it always welds in its hands.

Ingredients

  • 1 High School Senior
  • 1 cup Perpetual Confusion
  • 3 GPSs
  • 2 Sheets Class Schedule
  • 12 shots cheap "water"
  • 40 cans Overcompensation

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 F. Sift together the high school senior, perpetual confusion, and 1 GPS; set aside.
  2. In a medium bowl, cream together the remaining GPSs with the class schedules. Stir in the cheap "water" and overcompensation. Gradually blend in with the ingredients from Step 1. Press the paste into the human mold.
  3. Bake for 12 to 15 minutes in the preheated oven, or until the Freshman is golden brown along the crust. Remove from tray at once to cool on wire racks.
