The Sophomore

Description

This variety of college student typically lives in The Library, scavenging in preparation for Final Exam season. This creature's greatest predator is The Organic Chemistry Professor.

Ingredients

  • 1 Freshman
  • 2 cups Imposter Syndrome
  • 100 g Fashion Sense
  • 1 set Airpods
  • 8 Roommates

Steps

  1. Mix together the Imposter Syndrome, fashion sense, Airpods, and roommates. Stir well. Pour marinade over the Freshman. Cover, and refrigerate for at least 2 hours, basting at least twice.
  2. Preheat oven to 400 F.
  3. Cook the Sophomore for 1 hour in the preheated oven. After the first hour, remove the aluminum foil. Baste, reduce heat to 325 F, and continue roasting for 1 more hour. Let rest for about 30 minutes before plating.
